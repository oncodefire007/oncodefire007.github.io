Our Services

Quality, Dependable, and Affordable.

24-Hour Emergency Service

Oncode Fire and safety inc. is your partner in continued fire protection by offering affordable maintenance services to make sure your systems are running at peak performance at all times. Our dedicated staff is here for you 24 hours with reliable emergency service.

Guaranteed Best Prices in GTA

Our multi-faceted operation offers inspections, parts, maintenance and installations. We will provide prompt and efficient service to meet all of your safety needs. We also offer free consulting service. Schedule an inspection today!

Fire Extinguishers & Fire Hoses

Annual certifications of fire extinguishers on NFPA-10 and the current Ontario Fire Code. Annual inspections of fire hoses, on NFPA-1962 and the current Ontario Fire Code. A monthly visual inspection of the equipment. Provide monthly written inspection reports noting deficiencies and/or repairs.

Fire Alarm Systems

Annual inspections of fire alarm and detection system components on the Ontario Fire Code. An inspection report noting deficiencies and/or repairs. Installation of new fire alarm systems for new construction or existing building improvements.

Fire Pump & Sprinkler Systems

Annual tests and inspections of fire pumps, on NFPA-20 and the current Ontario Fire Code. Perform an annual test and inspection of the building sprinkler systems, on NFPA-25. Installation of new sprinkler systems for new construction and renovation projects. Provide a inspection report noting deficiencies and/or repairs.

Fire Suppression Systems

Semi-annual inspection. Provide an inspection report noting deficiencies and/or repairs.

Emergency Lights

Installation of Emergency lights. Annual and monthly test and inspection on the current Ontario Fire Code. Provide inspection reports noting deficiencies and/or repairs.

Smoke Alarm

Installation and inspection of smoke alarm system. An annual certification , as current Ontario Fire Code. Periodic inspections of the equipment. Provide a written inspection report noting deficiencies and/or repairs.

Duct Smoke Detector

Installation on furnace for shout down. Annual and monthly test and inspection on the current Ontario Fire Code. Provide inspection reports noting deficiencies and/or repairs.

Canadian Fire Alarm Association.

We are proud members of the Canadian Fire Alarm Association (CFAA) and have been certified and authorized to provide quality, dependable, and life-saving services to both the public and private sectors.
