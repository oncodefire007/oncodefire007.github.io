About us.

Oncode Fire and Safety Inc., a fast growing Fire protection service company with 20 year experience and advanced technology, focuses on coventional and intelligent Fire Alarm System installation, inspection and maintanence. Our company is proud to have a group of experienced, hard-working and responsible employees providing services to meet your fire protection needs.

Our high quality service makes us a company that you can trust and depend on. We can take care of all your fire protection issues so you won't have to worry about them and will be able to concentrate on making your company the best it can be. We realize that our customers are the cornerstone of our bussiness. We value our relationship of mutual respect and trust with our customers. That's why providing the most efficient service to our customer is our number one priority.
